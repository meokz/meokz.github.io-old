Light Field Blender

This research presents a near-eye light field display for augmented reality using Transmissive Mirror Device instead of conventional optical elements such as half-mirror, free-from optics, and waveguide.

Ars Electronica 2017で実機のデモ展示を行いました。SIGGRAPH Asia 2017で登壇発表を行いました。
